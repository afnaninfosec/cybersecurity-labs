## 📈 Key Trends (2026)

- Google dropped below 90% for first time since 2014
- AI search (Perplexity, ChatGPT) up 5x year-over-year
- ~60% of Google searches end with zero clicks (AI Overviews)
- Bing surged to 36% desktop share in Japan via Copilot
- Privacy engines plateaued after rapid 2019-2022 growth
